Welcome to Sky High University!

Brain, Brawn, and Beyond

Welcome superheroes and sidekicks to Sky High University. We currently live in a world filled with crime, villians, and all manner of corruption that threatens our very existence. Here, at Sky High, we train all the aspiring superheroes and sidekicks to be prepared for wahtever they may face in the real world. Whether it be a giant world ending volcano, or the deathlaser, we got you covered! Enroll today!


Safety First!

If you're concerned at all about your safety, rest assured that we take security very seriously. Sky High is in constant motion as a precaution against those who might have nefarious plans for the school. Students are transported to the school by special Flying Buses. (free admission) The school's location is supplied to only a few highly qualified individuals, such as Ron Wilson and the other bus drivers.
